Handhelds I Like

A webpage that shares some of my favorite video game handhelds.

Playdate

Things I Like

  • Pretty Sick Color - So many consoles are neutral-toned so a bright yellow console stands out and is really cool.
  • A Crank?! - A unique addition that allows for a wide variety of games not possible on other devices.
  • Being Encouraged to Create - Developing your own games for the platform is encouraged via its free to use SDK.
